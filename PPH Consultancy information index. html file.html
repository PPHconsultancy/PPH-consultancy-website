About Us

Established on June 20, 2024, PPH Professional Consultancy and Services is a dynamic, purpose-driven firm committed to improving the environmental and geographical aspects that directly impact our clients’ operations and communities. Our work spans the service industry, governmental and private institutions, and construction-affiliated companies, delivering tailored solutions that promote environmental sustainability, regulatory compliance, and strategic development.

At the heart of PPH is its founder—a seasoned professional with over a decade of hands-on experience in environmental assessment and auditing, GIS mapping and survey creation, data collection and analysis, training, project management, environmental policy, and research. Their unique blend of expertise makes PPH a holistic and forward-thinking consultancy.

Our day-to-day operations focus on climate change adaptation, energy and development solutions, ecological surveys, environmental assessments and audits, project design and execution, research, and training. Each project is approached with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and collaboration.

PPH also emphasizes capacity building through education and training, equipping both public and private sector clients with tools to meet modern environmental and disaster-related challenges. We collaborate extensively with government ministries, NGOs, private firms, and community stakeholders to foster sustainable progress through meaningful partnerships and professional networking.

At PPH, we believe in empowering change through knowledge, strategy, and practical solutions. Let us help you transform your vision into impact.
